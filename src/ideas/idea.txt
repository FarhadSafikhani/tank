

//GAME DESIGN

Pitch: A survivors style game, with Tanks. 
You enter short raids, defeat waves of enemies, beat the boss to win the raid.
Each run can make your permanently stronger.

Raid: 20 minute raids. Beat final boss of raid to get back to base. 
During raid enemies drop XP and lets your tank level up.

Enemies (Alien theme):
 - goobers (slow, low hp, melee)
 - scout (fast, low hp, melee)
 - grunt (slow, high hp, melee)
